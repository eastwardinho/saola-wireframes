Meet the Saola

Deep in the Annamite mountains, between Vietnam and Laos, lives one of the world's rarest large mammals. The Saola was unknown to science until 1992 ‚Äî one of the most spectacular zoological discoveries of the 20th century.

With its elegant horns and gentle eyes, the Saola is nicknamed the "Asian Unicorn." Fewer than 100 are believed to exist. It's a symbol of what's extraordinary hiding in plain sight.

"Vietnamese craftsmanship is like the Saola ‚Äî extraordinary, but hidden from the world. We're here to change that."
