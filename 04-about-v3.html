"We believe light transforms spaces into sanctuaries."

Saola was born from a simple observation: the most beautiful moments happen in the right light. A dinner with loved ones. A quiet evening reading. The soft glow that welcomes you home.

We set out to create lamps that do more than illuminate‚Äîthey create atmosphere, evoke emotion, and celebrate Vietnamese craftsmanship on a global stage.

What's in a Name

Saola

sao + la = star + leaf

Our name combines the Vietnamese words for "star" (sao) and "leaf" (la)‚Äîrepresenting light that's both celestial and grounded in nature. The Saola is also a rare, graceful antelope found only in Vietnam, symbolizing our commitment to uniqueness and Vietnamese heritage.
